# Shoex_RelationalDB_Dashboard
Visualization of Online shoe superstore company dataset in relational DB format

Key Takeways-

Total Sales Volume & Revenue:
Total quantity sold: 4846 units
Total revenue generated (sum of final price): 7.53M
Indicates overall business performance and revenue scale.
Sales Trend Over Time:
The line chart shows sales fluctuating with noticeable peaks in early 2021 and late 2021.
Helps identify seasonal trends or periods of high demand.
Top-performing Subcategories:
Bar chart shows Outdoor, Sandals, Loafers as top subcategories by revenue.
Provides insight into product portfolio performance for strategic focus.

Payment Methods Distribution:

Pie chart reveals Bank Transfer and Credit Card as dominant payment methods.
Useful for optimizing payment channels and promotions.
Sales Channel Analysis:
Online sales appear significant (filter applied on Online channel).
Suggests the importance of e-commerce strategies.
Geographic Insights:
Filter shows sales data from Norway (selected).
Indicates potential for country-specific marketing and expansion.
